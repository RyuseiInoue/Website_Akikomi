Android 11

The OS that gets to what’s important.

Go straight to the stuff that matters most. Because Android 11 is optimized for how you use your phone. Giving you powerful device controls. And easier ways to manage conversations, privacy settings and so much more.

Android 11 Highlights

Manage your conversations.

Get all your messages in one place.

See, respond to and control your conversations across multiple messaging apps. All in the same spot. Then select people that you always chat with. These priority conversations appear on your lock screen. So you never miss anything important.

Security updates, right from Google Play.

With Android 11, you get even more security and privacy fixes sent to your phone from Google Play. The same way that all your other apps update. So you get peace of mind. And your device stays armed with the most recent defence.
